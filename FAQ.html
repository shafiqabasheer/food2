Foodie - FAQ
How do I place an order?

You can place an order by browsing the menu, adding items to your cart, and proceeding to checkout.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept debit/credit cards, UPI, and cash on delivery (COD).

Can I track my order?

Yes, after placing your order you can track it in the 'Track Order' page.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 30-45 minutes depending on your location.

What if I face issues with my order?

You can contact our support team via the Contact page for quick help.
